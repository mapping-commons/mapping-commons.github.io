Mapping Commons

Mapping Commons pulls together mappings from various sources with the goal of offering them as simple to read, well provenanced TSV files in the SSSOM format. At first, the effort will focus on biomedical ontologies and controlled vocabularies, see for example the Mouse-Human Ontology Mapping Effort. The initiative was founded early 2021.
If you want to contribute mappings or have questions, please use the issue tracker.
